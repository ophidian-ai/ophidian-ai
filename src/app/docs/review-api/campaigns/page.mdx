# Review Request Campaigns

Review request campaigns allow you to email your customers asking them to leave a Google review. Campaigns are available on the **Pro tier** only.

---

## Overview

A campaign sends a branded email to your eligible customer contacts with a direct link to your Google review page. OphidianAI tracks which contacts clicked the link and attributes new reviews that arrived within 48 hours.

---

## Creating a Campaign

From the admin dashboard:

1. Navigate to **Review Management** and open a Pro tier client config.
2. Click **New Campaign** in the top right.
3. Fill in the campaign form:
   - **Client Config** — select the Pro tier config
   - **Campaign Name** — internal label (e.g. "Spring 2026 Review Drive")
   - **Review Link** — optional override. If blank, OphidianAI auto-fetches the Google review link from the GBP API.
4. Preview the email by clicking **Preview email**.
5. Click **Save as Draft** or **Send Now**.

---

## Contact Filtering

Campaigns are sent to contacts from the **Email Marketing** contact list for the same client, filtered as follows:

- `subscribed = true`
- `tags` contains `customer`
- Up to **500 contacts** per campaign (Pro tier limit)

To add contacts to the `customer` tag, use the Email Marketing contact manager or the `/api/email/contacts` endpoint.

---

## Review Link Resolution

The review link is resolved in this priority order:

1. **Manual override** — URL entered in the campaign form
2. **GBP API** — `newReviewUrl` from the location metadata (`/v1/accounts/{account}/locations/{location}`)
3. **Fallback** — Google search review URL constructed from the GBP location ID

---

## Email Template

The campaign email is a minimal branded HTML email:

- OphidianAI logo header
- Personalized greeting (first name)
- Call-to-action button linking to the review page
- Unsubscribe footer (required by CAN-SPAM)

The email is sent from `reviews@ophidianai.com` via Resend.

---

## Attribution Tracking

OphidianAI attributes new reviews to campaigns using a 48-hour attribution window:

- The window opens at the campaign `sent_at` timestamp
- New reviews that appear in the config's review feed within that window are counted as attributed
- Attribution is approximate — exact match requires click tracking (planned for a future release)

The `reviews_attributed` count is updated daily by the attribution cron job at `0 8 * * *`.

---

## Campaign Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `draft` | Created but not sent |
| `scheduled` | Queued for future send (not yet implemented) |
| `sent` | Emails dispatched |
| `completed` | Attribution window closed |
