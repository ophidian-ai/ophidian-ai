# FAQ

## What do the scores mean?

Each category is scored on a 1–5 scale:

| Score | Color | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1–2 | Red | Needs immediate attention |
| 3 | Yellow | Functional but has room to improve |
| 4–5 | Green | Performing well |

Scores are relative to best practices for that category, not compared against competitors. A score of 5 means the site meets or exceeds all key criteria in that area.

---

## How are rankings tracked?

Rankings are reported as visibility tiers — directional estimates of where your site appears in search results, not exact position numbers.

Exact positions vary constantly based on the searcher's location, device, browser history, and personalization signals. Tiers give a stable, meaningful signal: are you on the first page, second page, or not showing up at all?

| Tier | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| Top 3 | Strong first-page visibility |
| Top 10 | First page of results |
| Top 20 | Second page of results |
| Not Found | Not appearing in the top 20 results |

Tiers are checked using search engine results pages, not browser-specific or personalized sessions.

## How often are reports generated?

Monthly, on the **1st of each month**. Each report covers audit findings, keyword trends, and recommendations for the prior month.

Reports are delivered to your dashboard and by email. You can download past reports at any time from the Monthly Reports section of your dashboard.

---

## Can I run an audit on demand?

Not through the client dashboard. Audits run automatically on the monthly cycle.

If you need an out-of-cycle audit — for example, after a major site update — contact your account manager. Admins can trigger a manual audit from the admin panel.

---

## How does GBP draft approval work?

1. OphidianAI generates Google Business Profile post copy as part of your monthly audit.
2. Drafts appear in your dashboard with a **Pending** status.
3. Review the draft. If the content looks good, click **Approve**.
4. Copy the approved text and paste it directly into your Google Business Profile to publish it.

OphidianAI never posts to your GBP directly. You control everything that gets published.

---

## What is AI Visibility?

AI Visibility measures whether your business appears when people search using AI-powered tools like Google AI Overview, ChatGPT search, and Perplexity.

More searches are now answered directly by AI without the user clicking a traditional link. If your site is not being referenced by these tools, you are missing an increasingly important traffic source.

The AI Visibility score reflects how well your content is structured and positioned to appear in AI-generated answers.

---

## What happens when a GBP draft expires?

Drafts that are not approved within **14 days** expire automatically. An expired draft cannot be approved.

A new draft is generated with your next monthly audit. If you want to publish content in the meantime, you can write your own post directly in your Google Business Profile — no approval needed on your end.
