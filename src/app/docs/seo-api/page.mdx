# Getting Started

OphidianAI SEO gives you a monthly audit of your website's search health, keyword visibility tracking, issue detection, and ready-to-post Google Business Profile copy — all in one dashboard.

## What the SEO Product Does

- Audits your website across six categories and scores each 1–5
- Tracks how visible your site is for target keywords in search results
- Surfaces technical, content, and local SEO issues with plain-language explanations
- Generates a branded PDF report delivered on the 1st of each month
- Drafts Google Business Profile posts for your review and approval
- Monitors your presence in AI-powered search experiences (Google AI Overview, ChatGPT, Perplexity)

## How to Read Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is organized into sections:

**Audit Scores** — Six category scores shown as colored cards. Each score is 1–5. A trend arrow shows whether the score improved or declined since the previous audit.

**Keyword Visibility** — A table of your tracked keywords with their current visibility tier, whether they appear in AI Overviews, and the best-ranking competitor for that term.

**Issues Found** — A list of specific findings from the audit, grouped by area, with a severity rating and explanation of why it matters.

**Monthly Reports** — An archive of all past PDF reports with download links.

**GBP Drafts** — Google Business Profile post copy we generated for you. Review each draft and click Approve to mark it ready. You copy-paste the content directly into your GBP profile.

**AI Insights** — A written narrative summarizing the audit, patterns we noticed, and what to focus on next.

## Score Color Coding

Scores use a consistent color system across all six categories:

| Score | Color | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1–2 | Red | Needs immediate attention |
| 3 | Yellow | Room for improvement |
| 4–5 | Green | In good shape |

## Score Categories Explained

| Category | What It Measures |
|----------|-----------------|
| **On-Page** | Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, keyword usage |
| **Technical** | Crawlability, indexation, structured data, redirects, canonical tags |
| **Content** | Page depth, readability, topical coverage, thin or duplicate content |
| **Local** | NAP consistency, Google Business Profile completeness, local citations |
| **Speed** | Core Web Vitals, page load time, image optimization, render-blocking resources |
| **AI Visibility** | Presence in Google AI Overview, ChatGPT, and Perplexity search results |

## Keyword Visibility Tiers

Keyword positions are reported as visibility tiers, not exact positions. These are directional estimates based on where your site appears in search engine results pages.

| Tier | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Top 3** | Your site appears in the top 3 results — strong visibility |
| **Top 10** | Your site appears on the first page of results |
| **Top 20** | Your site appears on the second page of results |
| **Not Found** | Your site was not found in the top 20 results for this keyword |

Positions can vary based on location, device, and personalization. Treat tiers as relative benchmarks, not precise rankings.

## Report Delivery Schedule

Monthly PDF reports are generated automatically on the **1st of each month**. Each report covers the prior month's audit findings, keyword trends, and recommendations.

Reports appear in the **Monthly Reports** section of your dashboard as soon as they are generated. You will also receive an email notification when your report is ready.

## GBP Draft Workflow

1. OphidianAI generates Google Business Profile post copy as part of your monthly audit.
2. Drafts appear in the **GBP Drafts** section of your dashboard with a **Pending** status.
3. Review the draft content. If it looks good, click **Approve**.
4. Copy the approved content and paste it into your Google Business Profile to publish.
5. Drafts that are not approved within 14 days expire automatically. A new draft is generated with your next monthly audit.

You are always in control of what gets published to your GBP. OphidianAI never posts directly to your profile.
